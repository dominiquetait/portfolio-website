About Me

DEMOGRAPHICAL INFORMATION

I'm a 21-year-old female from South Africa, currently studying full-time at Swinburne University.

HOMETOWN

I was born in Durban, South Africa, and grew up between Durban and Johannesburg before moving to Australia in 2012. After living in Brisbane and Perth, I settled in Melbourne in 2018, where I currently reside. My hometown, Durban, is a vibrant coastal city known for its golden beaches along the Indian Ocean, year-round subtropical climate, and being home to the busiest port in Africa. It's also famous for its rich cultural mix, Indian cuisine - especially the iconic 'bunny chow' - and being a hub for surfing and outdoor activities.

GREATEST ACHIEVEMENT

One of my greatest achievements so far has been completing my first year of university while also designing and developing my first small game project. Balancing full-time study with a creative pursuit not only strengthened my time management skills but also gave me the confidence to pursue a career in games development.

INTERESTS

Some of my favourite games include Fallout, Minecraft, Helldivers 2, Destiny 2, and Marvel Rivals. I don't read much for leisure, but I do enjoy diving into books when they're about topics I want to learn more about, such as programming or design. When it comes to films, I like action movies such as Baby Driver and the Marvel Cinematic Universe, though I tend to enjoy TV series even more. I'm especially drawn to mystery, crime, and drama shows like Suits, Found, The Rookie, House M.D., and Psych, as well as comedies like Brooklyn 99 and Friends.
